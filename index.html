What Your Agent Can Do
📧

email_agent.inbox()

Sort, filter, draft replies. Your agent processes email while you sleep. Flag urgent items, archive noise, draft responses.

🌐

browser_agent.navigate()

Full browser automation. Fill forms, check prices, monitor pages, scrape data. Headless Chromium with stealth plugins.

🔔

monitor_agent.watch()

Watch system metrics, API endpoints, website changes. Get alerts via Telegram, Discord, or Signal when thresholds break.

💾

memory_agent.recall()

Persistent long-term memory. Vector search across all past conversations. Your agent remembers context across sessions.

🔧

tool_agent.extend()

Write custom tools in Python. Your agent loads them dynamically. Connect to any API, database, or service.

🔒

security.local_only()

Everything runs on your hardware. No data leaves your network. Full disk encryption. Zero-trust architecture.
